What is HoW


HoW stands for House of Wisdom, which is an advanced platform that offers online tutoring and education solutions, House of Wisdom was an intellectual center in Baghdad, Iraq back in Iraq's golden age.

The Idea of HoW


HoW academy has built a society for scientists, scholars and collected academics to research and study alchemy, which was later used to create the structure of modern chemistry. We as a team adopted the same idea by providing this platform to collect academies and individuals to share their knowledge and skills to escalate education status into a new level.
